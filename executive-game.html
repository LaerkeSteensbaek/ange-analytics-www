Linergame Executive

A 1-day interactive session aimed specifically at senior executives in the container shipping industry. The session will be tailored to match the skill levels of the participant group. The session will provide a clear hands-on learning experience of the fundamental challenges in relation to process management, information flow, forecasting and planning necessary in a modern container shipping line.

Ideal for: Board members as well as Senior managers in container shipping lines, freight forwarders, ports and terminals
