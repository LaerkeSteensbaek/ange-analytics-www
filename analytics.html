In Brief

With the use BI algorithms, we created an interactive forecasting tool for DAMCO, a subsidiary of Maersk.

The tool generates a report every week and includes:

Volume Prediction Charts.

Volume Prediction by Carriers.

Prediction Accuracy Graphs.

Carrier Allocation Charts.

Place of Receipt distribution Charts.

Place of Delivery distribution Charts.

The Benefits

Give users an overview and simplifies the decision making process.

The Challenges

To clean, aggregate and design the master data.
